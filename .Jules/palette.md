## 2026-06-24 - Added Accessibility Tooltips to Icon-Only Buttons
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons often lack accessible names, making them difficult for screen reader users to understand. The tooltip property on IconButton provides a simple and effective way to ensure accessibility while improving the UX for all users with a hover interaction.
**Action:** Always add 'tooltip' properties to IconButton widgets that do not have adjacent visible text explaining their action.
